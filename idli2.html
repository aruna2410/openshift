IDLI

It is one of the best south indian dish. It is very simple to prepare. Now let us check the preparation of idli

Ingredients are:

1. Rice flour 2. ground urad dal 4. salt 5. fenugreek seeds

process:

Idli preparation is very easy. First we need to prepare a batter for making idli. So we must soak all the ingredients over 5 hours. After that we grind the soaked ingredients. Then we will keep it for 3 hours. Then we will prepare idli with that batter by using idli vessel.

Tips:

It is very tasty when we eat idli with sambar or coconut chutney.
